Plays at Ranga Shankara
The popular theatre hosts several plays all through the year. You can watch a diverse range of productions in Kannada as well as in English too.

Wildlife Safari
One of the best places in Jodhpur to observe nature in its entire raw wilderness is at Bannerghatta National Park. The park houses animals in its zoo, rescue centre.

Margarita Festival
Trust Fox in the Field to celebrate all things in life that matter, i.e Margaritas. Grab margaritas of all kinds with some great food here.

Parasailing
This is one of the most sought-after activities to do in Jodhpur, especially for those who enjoy adventure and a good adrenaline rush.

Skandagiri Treks
Embark on one of the most popular trekking destinations near Jodhpur, Skandagiri Hills, and have an adventurous day

Wine Tour
Enjoy guided wine tour that takes you to a renowned winery in Jodhpur where you can learn about the process of making wine and how vineyards were formed.

Microlight Flying
There is no better experience than going on a microlight flight above the city. It is considered one of the most offbeat things to do in Jodhpur.
